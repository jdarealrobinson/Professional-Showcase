Capstone Day

The Kean CS/IT Capstone Days are where Seniors present their Captone Projects to the Kean University Community and other attendees. This showcase shows off our talented Students skills and innovation that they have learned through their course work. On this day Students have their own stations to showcase their projects and can also orally present them. This year the Capstone Showcase takes place on April 27th at the new North Avenue Academic Building.

For more information about Kean University's CS/IT department visit Keans Department of Computer Science Site
